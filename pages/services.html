DROP TRAILER PROJECTS

Avillion also offers logistics services for DROP TRAILER CAPACITY, which is a method of shipment and transport where drivers bring their trailer shipment to a warehouse and “drop” it off there. One driver can then hook up their truck to a new trailer that warehouse workers already loaded and prepared. DROP TRAILER PROJECTS allow for more efficiency and flexibility in the supply chain, as drivers do not have to wait for loading or unloading and can move on to their next destination faster. DROP TRAILER PROJECTS also reduce detention fees, driver wait time, and congestion at the docks.
