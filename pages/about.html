Our story

Avillion LLC is a logistics company based in St. Louis, MO, that was founded by three young professionals with different backgrounds but a common vision: to create a reliable, efficient and innovative freight broker service that can handle any transportation needs. The founders are:

Adnan Imamovic, a software developer and accountant who has a passion for creating smart solutions for complex problems. He is the registered agent and the owner of Avillion LLC.

Daniel E. Heco, an architectural engineer who has a flair for creating and managing projects and structures. He is the Accounts Director, Sales and the head of business development of Avillion LLC.

Benjamin Zelihic, a passionate english linguist who has a knack for designing and optimizing systems and processes as a software developer as well. He is is the chief operations officer and the head of quality control of Avillion LLC.

The three met at a local meetup for entrepreneurs and innovators, where they discovered their shared interest in logistics and transportation. They decided to join forces and start their own company, which they named Avillion LLC, after the word “pikoura”, which means “three” in Maori, a language spoken by the indigenous people of New Zealand.

Avillion LLC was officially launched on November 21, 2022, with a mission to provide transportation services for vans, flatbeds, reefers, dry bulk, tankers and coal/coke2. The company uses advanced technology and data analytics to find the best carriers and routes for each shipment, ensuring that the goods are delivered on time and in good condition. The company also offers competitive rates and personalized customer service to its clients.

One of the first clients of Avillion LLC was Castle Tires ,a recycling service center located in Portland, OR. Castle Tire LLC needed a reliable freight forwarder to handle their shipments of raw materials and finished products across the country. Avillion LLC impressed them with their professionalism, efficiency and innovation, and soon became their preferred logistics partner.

Another client of Avillion LLC was Enza Inc, a trucking company that operates in various states. Enza Inc needed a freight broker to help them find more loads and optimize their routes. Avillion LLC provided them with a user-friendly platform that allowed them to access real-time information on available loads, rates and locations. Avillion LLC also helped them improve their fuel efficiency and safety by using smart algorithms and GPS tracking.

Avillion LLC has grown rapidly since its inception, thanks to its loyal customers and its dedicated team. The company now has more than 17 employees, including drivers, dispatchers, account managers and support staff. The company also has a network of more than 500 carriers across the country, covering all types of cargo and modes of transportation.

Avillion LLC is not only a successful business, but also a socially responsible one. The company supports various causes and charities in its community, such as environmental protection, education and health care. The company also promotes diversity and inclusion in its workplace, hiring people from different backgrounds, cultures and experiences.

Avillion LLC is a story of passion and innovation, of three young professionals who turned their dream into reality. The company is still growing and evolving, always looking for new ways to improve its services and satisfy its customers. Avillion LLC is not just a logistics company, but a logistics solution.
